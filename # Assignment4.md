## Additional notes to play game for marker

* When you start the game, there is gun on your right.
* regular enemy cannot attack on you. you will get damage when you hit regular enemy after 5 seconds you will get damage if you again hit regular enemy.
* Patrol enemy can patrol in all the groung and also can attack you and will follow for some range.
* you can press mouse button to fire butllet.
* you can use your inventory by pressing number key according to inventory box. if you want to use health box from your second inventory box, you can just press 2.
* if you use power up, player would run insted walk for 10 sec.
* you cannot pick up new item if your inventory has not any space available.You must have use any of them if you want to pick up new item.
* you can use key after pressing number key acoording to your inventory box. if you don't approach near by trophy you can not use key but you will get message to find trophy. You can use key near by tropy and game will end.
